Did a Tornado Touch Down in San Francisco?

The Full Story

On December 14, residents in San Francisco reported unusually strong winds, leading to speculation about a possible tornado. While rare in the Bay Area, certain atmospheric conditions can lead to these events. Meteorologists are currently analyzing the data to determine whether the phenomenon was indeed a tornado or just powerful gusts from a passing storm system.
